## The Limitations of Prompt Engineering
Prompt engineering has been a crucial aspect of AI development, but it's becoming increasingly clear that it's a low-level commodity. As the complexity of AI systems grows, the need for more sophisticated and scalable solutions becomes apparent. This is where Agentic OS comes in – by orchestrating specialized agents with clear boundaries, we can create more efficient, flexible, and observable systems.

## The Power of File-Based State
One of the key advantages of Agentic OS is its use of file-based state, such as Markdown handshakes. This approach ensures observability and debugging, allowing developers to track changes and identify issues more easily. In contrast, brittle API wrappers can lead to opaque and inflexible systems that are difficult to maintain.

## The Agentic Agent Runtime Concept
At the heart of Agentic OS is the Agentic Agent Runtime concept, which combines an immutable engine with declarative behaviors. This approach enables the creation of modular, reusable, and composable agents that can be easily integrated into larger systems. By leveraging this concept, developers can build more robust, scalable, and maintainable AI solutions.
